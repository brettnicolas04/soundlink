# SoundLink 

**Where underground electronic music meets community.**

A location-based platform connecting underground electronic music fans — starting with the minimal/micro house scene in Barcelona — built as a full business plan from idea through financial modelling, validation, and live investor-style defense.

> Final grade: 9/10 (ESADE Business School, Business Plan FDP)

---

## The problem

Minimal house fans are deeply connected to the music but disconnected from each other. Spotify gives you the sound, Resident Advisor gives you events, Instagram gives you content... nothing gives you the people. Validated through 18 field interviews, 6 venue conversations, and 44 survey responses in Barcelona.

## The solution

A genre-specific, location-based platform that filters people, content, and opportunities by sub-genre, shared DJ references, and location. Turning individual listening into local community.

- **Free tier**: full connection, discovery, and community access (never capped)
- **Premium (€7.99/mo)**: standing within the community, hosting rights, exclusive courses, artist events, discounts
- **Growth model**: a targeted referral chain (not broad marketing) — Artists → Passionate Listeners → Casual Listeners

## Key numbers (Year 1 projection)

- Revenue: €30,721 | Net profit: €1,302 | Gross margin: 96%
- LTV/CAC: 17.6x (vs. 3x industry benchmark)
- Initial capital required: €15,000 — fully self-funded, no external raise in Year 1

## What I learned

The hardest part of this project wasn't the platform; it was proving that a community would actually form and sustain itself. Building the financial model taught me to stress-test my own assumptions rather than defend them, and the biggest structural insight was realizing that user growth wasn't one risk among several. It is the single variable everything else in the model depends on.

## Status

Moving from academic plan to real-world validation — testing the referral-chain growth model with a live pilot before any further build investment.
